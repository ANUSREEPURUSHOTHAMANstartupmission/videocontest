About

How can we contribute to inspiring young innovators to become successful entrepreneurs? This video contest may become your first step in taking an action and making a difference in your locality, community, society, and the world by sharing your ideas from wherever you are. KSUM is excited to organize the “Inspire for Innovators” video contest. We invite all Startups and Students from the IEDC network to participate. Share your ideas about how you can inspire young innovators in your locality, community, society, and the world from the perspectives of the Innovation network.

Evaluation Committee

Panel of experts

As part of the contest, KSUM would provide

  • KSUM Certificate for all video entries.
  • The shortlisted videos will be screened at the Awards Ceremony as part of Procurement Summit 2023
  • Eligibility
  • Guidelines
  • Anyone interested in video production can join the contest
  • Must be able to participate in the Awards Ceremony on April 26th, 2023.
  • Individual or group entries are both welcome. Please note that only one entry per individual or group can be submitted.

What can you do to inspire young innovators to become successful entrepreneurs?

  • Focus on a social issue that our society is facing and try to provide at least one recommendation or innovative solution through your video.
  • Decide how to deliver your ideas, project, and creations! We welcome any creative ways to present your ideas, including presentations, animations, or other forms, for example, that incorporate original songs, dances, poems, or plays. You can also exhibit projects that you developed, such as tools, robots, or software.

Video Requirements

Language

You can submit in English, Malayalam, or silent (no dialogue or narration). (For Malayalam, please make sure to add English subtitles and vice versa)

Quality

High-quality video is encouraged (The video quality can range from 4K to lower, including videos made on smartphones).

Length

Up to 3 minutes, Minimum of 2 minutes

File size

Up to 400MB

Format

.mp4, .mov
